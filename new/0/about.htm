About

What is Play By Lyrics?
This site enables you to type in songtexts (or 'lyrics') from songs and automatically find the music video for it. You can type in whatever you remember of the song, and it will find it! If you know the artist or song title, adding this behind or before the lyrics usually enhances the results.
Some examples:
when I ruled the world
Paint black heart
loosing control come on rock everybody

Many people nowadays know how to do this manually with Google, but I thought this could be done better. This site:
  • Is faster than doing it yourself;
  • can try another result with just one click; and
  • has some extra features like auto-replay or downloading the mp3.
